SOCIAL JUSTICE PROJECT

Introduction

Our society nowadays is a medicatic one.

Many things we talk, think and care about come from what we see in the media. It provides information, entertainment, an escape, pleasure, but it can also cause harm.
We could divide the media into three major elements: the producers, the audience and the content. “Production involves anything having to do with the creation and distribution of mediated messages: how the messages are assembled, by whom, in what circumstances, and under what constraints. Content emphasizes the mediated messages themselves: what they present, and how; what is included, and by implication, what is excluded. Audience addresses the people who engage, consume, or interact with mediated messages: how they use the media, what sense they make of media content, and how they are affected by the media.”

Motivation

We consume media everywhere.

Media plays an important role in our daily lives since we consume media everywhere we are and sometimes do not manage to realize the power it has to perceive the world around us in a certain way. One important aspect of the media is stereotypes. Stereotypes reflect our beliefs that the few traits we stress are representative when applied to a group of people. There is a denial of the individual characteristics among the persons. Often stereotypes come in hand with prejudice, which Healey and O’Brien defined as “the tendency of an individual to think about other groups in negative ways, to attach negative emotions to those groups, and to prejudge individuals on the basis of their group membership”. Throughout the years, magazine covers have featured these stereotypes fostering a prejudice among society and using the power of that type of media for bad.

Goal

We can use the power of media for good.

If we can identify and be aware of the ways in which the media manipulates our viewing and judgement, we can revert that effect. Especially knowing and understanding the power of media, it can become a tool for spreading a good message too. Media can drive people together. It can do good. Through a Google Translate one can communicate in a foreign country or even a social cause can have a greater impact through Instagram. We focus on a specific type of media: magazine covers. In the Online Museum we show a column of covers that throughout the years have tried to use the power of media to convey a vanguardistas and positive message to the audience. We hope that through the online submission in the Cover-Section we will be able to continue the trend.
